Hello, we are three final year master's students who have designed this web/mobile application as a proof of concept for an electronic form submission system for A&E. Here we have created a a user-friendly front-end that will allow patients to fill in an ailment form that is submitted to the EN-HS server. This form will be checked by a receptionist and processed by the system backend to produce a list of relevant diagnoses for a triage doctor to view and confirm and give a priority level to so that a patient may be seen. Once the form is processed, an email receipt is issued to the patient, confirming that their form has been seen and approved.
