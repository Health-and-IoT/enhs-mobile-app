Hello , We are three university students who have designed this web application/mobile application as a Proof on concept. Where we have created an nhs symptom that will allow patients to fill in a symptom form then it will be submitted to a database which is held on a server.The receptionist will be able to retrive the form to check it has been completed and filled in correctly. The form will be then placed back on database on the server. Once the doctor has pulled the form the symptom algorithm detector will be ran to determine possible ailments and then the doctor can confirm and set a priority level.
